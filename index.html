Features of PS4

Remote play

Stream your PS4 games on PC, MAC or PS VITA over WiFi Just plug a PS4 or any controller into USB port of your PC or MAC.

Extended Storage

Backup your save files with an external hard drive or SSD. Upto 8TB of USB storage devices are compatible giving an incredible amount of space to save and organize your games.

Supersampling Mode

Enhance your gaming experience with enhanced graphics without a 4kTV.This mode renders at 4k resolution and downscales to 1080p to improve image quality.

Data Transfer

Easily transfer your data including saves, setting and downloaded games between new console and old console.You can easily transfer data with a LAN cable, router and internet.
